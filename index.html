Marc Gillioz

I am a theoretical physicist with industry experience in software development. I am currently as senior scientist in the Electrical Energy Efficiency Group at the HES-SO Valais-Wallis, in my home town of Sion, Switzerland.

High-energy physics

I originally graduated in high-energy physics, with a Ph.D. thesis on the Higgs boson and the possibility that it is a composite particle. After that I was a postdoc at the University of Southern Denmark, at UC Davis (USA), EPFL (Switzerland), and at SISSA (Italy). I became an expert in conformal field theory, a special case of quantum field theory with scale invariance in which we have a decent (and growing) understanding of the strong-coupling regime.
